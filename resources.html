Frequently Ask Questions

Are GrowUp services prepaid?

Yes. If you want to hire an employee, Growup Technologies requires receipt of the first month’s invoice before the employee starts work.

How do I communicate with my employee?

This is your decision. You can communicate via phone, e-mail, instant messengers, video conferencing, zoom call, and/ or Skype.

What are the leaves and holiday policy?

Saturdays and Sundays (as per IST) are permanent off for the VA. In addition to this, he/she can take an additional 2 days off per month to be approved by the client. The client has to assign tasks accordingly as Saturdays and Sundays are OFF.

What are tasks a VA can perform?

There are a lot of tasks a VE can perform. There are 2 types of VA:

  • Generic VE
  • Specific VE
    • Generic VE consists of jobs like inbox management, personal assistance, email marketing, Govt. form filling, financial services, etc. Specific VA consists of jobs like Linkedin handling, Digital marketing, Content writing, Website designing, etc.

    Are there any hidden charges?

    No, There are no hidden charges associated with VA. You just have to pay charges as per the plan.

    Is India the best outsourcing destination?

    India, with a 65% share, has the largest percentage of the global offshore outsourcing market. In addition, India’s outsourcing industry is valued at US$70 billion per annum andIndia has been consistently ranked the number one outsourcing destination for several years. From its stable democracy, English-speaking professionals, large technical talent pool, and hard work ethic, India, in every respect, is the number one outsourcing destination.

    How are employees supervised at your end?

    The employes are supervised directly at our end but GrowUp tends to take monthly feedback from both client and VA.

    What type of IT will be in place?

    In terms of software requirements you are more than welcome to provide any software you wish your employee to work with.

      Your employee will be fully equipped:
    • Their own laptop
    • Printer and scanner
    • Commonly used softwares
    • Peripherals such as webcam, headset etc
    • Broadband internet connection

    Is there a chance my virtual employee will leave?

    There are a very little possibility that your VE will leave the job. The attrition rate is negligible. But if any VE leaves, we will substitute him with another VE.

    How can i check what my employee has done for the day?

    You can ask your employee to email you a daily status report in any form you want. These may include text files of daily report sheets, visual updates (sometimes known as "before and after" images), Web 2.0 docs or other updates that you may require.

    Can i offer incentive to my employee?

    Yes. Any incentives you offer will be passed through our standard accounting process in reaching your Employee. However all such incentive schemes should only be discussed with our Consultants who will brief your employee. This ensures all our staff remain neutral to greed and ensures we maintain an equilibrium amongst all our employees.
